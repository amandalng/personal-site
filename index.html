Hi there! I'm Amanda,

and I'm glad you're here.

Here's my life in a nutshell:

0/ I was born and bred in Jakarta as a (pseudo) third culture kid who attended an international school and chose to compete at sports that were probably not the best fit for a girl so height-challenged

1/ I went to UC Berkeley where I majored in Economics and minored in (my best academic decision to date) Global Poverty & Practice, graduating with good grades without rolling down 4.0 Hill like a fool

2/ I dabbled in work tweeting with other little birdies, supporting financial inclusion efforts in Indonesia, and coercing Berkeley kids to make their best academic decision to date

3/ I made a new home in Singapore spending my days eating chicken rice, pizza, nasi lemak, and Korean fried chicken, occasionally running to avoid waking up one day 3x what I currently weigh

4/ I kicked off my career as a management consultant at A.T. Kearney, where I spent two years roaming Southeast Asia helping big corporations solve their toughest operational and top-line growth issues

5/ I turned my passion project into a company called Origo Edu, an education consultancy that helps to fill a knowledge/resource/culture gap that exists in counseling systems across Indonesian high schools

6/ I became a (dog) mom to the world's cutest Australian Labradoodle, who now has a higher like:follower ratio than I do (follow him today @olliedood__!)

I am passionate about social impact, technology, and storytelling, and am always on the lookout for an interesting conversation or opportunity to do or build something cool. If you'd like to chat, hit me up!
